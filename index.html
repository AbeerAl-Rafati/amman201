Eating (also known as consumption) is the intake of food, and a heterogeneous organism is usually provided with energy and allowed for growth. Animals and other heterotrophs must eat to survive - carnivores eat other animals, herbivores eat plants, carnivores consume a mixture of both plant and animal matter, and predators eat detritus. Fungi digest organic matter outside their bodies, unlike animals that digest their food inside their bodies. For humans, eating is an activity of daily life. Some individuals may limit the amount of food they eat. This may be a result of choosing a lifestyle, due to hunger or starvation, as part of a diet or as a religious fast.

And it became known internationally, and pizza is a main meal as well as an appetizer.
